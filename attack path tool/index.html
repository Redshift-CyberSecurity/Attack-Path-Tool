Place Node: Shift + Click
Link Nodes: Shift + Click on Node, drag to another Node.
Change Label: Shift + Click on Node.
Change Position of Label: Arrow Keys.
Mark Link/Node: Click Link/Node. Press M.
Move Link to Front: Click Link. Press F.
Move Graph: Click and drag on open space.
Delete Link/Node: Click Link/Node, Press DEL/BACKSPACE.
Show/Snap to Grid: Press G.
